STONE OUTPOST — CLEAR GUIDE (Level 45)
=======================================

Type:    Fixed Bandit Camp (always visible on world map)
Zone:    Oak Forest / T2 region
Reset:   Every 7 days after first attack
Loot:    Common + Uncommon Blueprint Tubes, T2-T3 resources
Purpose: Best F2P source of Common/Uncommon tubes at Lv.45


=======================================
 REQUIREMENTS
=======================================

  Item              | Minimum      | Recommended at Lv.45
  ------------------|--------------|---------------------------
  Player Level      | 30+          | You're 45 -- no issue
  Primary Weapon    | T2 firearm   | Pepperbox or Bronze Rifle
  Backup Weapon     | Any T2       | Spare Pistol or Spear
  Armor             | T2 full set  | Full T2 Leather Set
  Healing           | Some food    | Ointments + food + bandages
  Durability        | 70%+ minimum | 100% repaired before going

  NOTE: At Lv.45 you are overqualified for this camp.
  The main risk is not dying -- it is running out of healing
  or weapon durability mid-fight. Prepare accordingly.


=======================================
 INVENTORY CHECKLIST
=======================================

  WEAPONS (2 slots):
  [ ] Pepperbox or Bronze Rifle     -- 100% durability, repaired
  [ ] Backup T2 Pistol or Spear     -- emergency if primary breaks

  ARMOR (worn, 5 slots):
  [ ] Leather Hat                   -- 100% durability
  [ ] Leather Jacket                -- 100% durability
  [ ] Jeans (Pants)                 -- 100% durability
  [ ] Leather Boots                 -- 100% durability
  [ ] Leather Gloves                -- 100% durability

  HEALING (5-6 slots):
  [ ] 3x Ointment                   -- BOSS ONLY, don't waste early
  [ ] 5x Grilled Meat / Cactus Fruit-- use on regular bandits
  [ ] 2x Bandage                    -- emergency top-up between fights

  TOOLS (1-2 slots):
  [ ] 2x Pickaxe                    -- grab ore on return trip
  [ ] 1x Axe                        -- optional, timber nodes nearby

  INVENTORY SPACE:
  [ ] Leave 15-20 slots EMPTY       -- loot is heavy (ingots, tubes, tools)

  LEAVE AT HOME:
  > Raw ore, timber, excess mats    -- wastes loot slots
  > More than 2 weapons             -- unnecessary
  > Anything below 70% durability   -- will break mid-fight


=======================================
 PRE-RAID CHECKLIST (Do Before Leaving)
=======================================

  [ ] Repair ALL weapons to 100% at Repair Bench
  [ ] Repair ALL armor pieces to 100%
  [ ] Craft/stock ointments and food -- don't go short on healing
  [ ] Clear your inventory of everything not on the list
  [ ] Park Wagon near camp entrance -- use for overflow loot hauls
  [ ] Check Anger Meter -- do NOT leave if meter is near full
      (bandit raid may hit your ranch while you're away)
  [ ] Check ranch production queues -- start Foundry before leaving


=======================================
 HOW TO FIND THE CAMP
=======================================

  1. Open World Map (globe icon)
  2. Zoom out and scan for a skull/camp icon in the Oak Forest area
  3. Stone Outpost appears as a PERMANENT marker -- always visible
  4. Tap the icon to see recommended level and gear tier
  5. Travel there directly -- no Stash Map needed


=======================================
 COMBAT STRATEGY
=======================================

  PHASE 1 -- Clear Regular Bandits (Do NOT rush boss)
  ----------------------------------------------------
  > Use Rifle: shoot from maximum range, pull 1-2 enemies at a time
  > Use cover: hide behind walls/rocks between shots to reload/heal
  > Kill ranged bandits (guns) BEFORE melee bandits
  > Use food items here -- save ointments for the boss
  > Watchdogs: kill first if they detect you, they alert the camp

  PHASE 2 -- Boss Fight (Blasting Gary / Hal)
  --------------------------------------------
  BOSS MECHANICS:
    > Throws DYNAMITE -- AoE damage, never stand still
    > Can HEAL himself -- stops healing when HP drops below 50%
    > Hits harder than regular bandits -- don't tank his shots

  STRATEGY:
    > ATTACK ONLY after all regular bandits are dead
    > Shoot > immediately move sideways > shoot again (hit-and-run)
    > When he throws dynamite: sprint away, wait for explosion, return
    > At ~50% HP: he stops healing -- push maximum damage here
    > Use Ointments NOW if health drops below half
    > Pistol headshots = fastest kill on boss

  PHASE 3 -- Loot
  ---------------
  > Boss killed > Leader's Chest spawns > open THIS FIRST (best loot)
  > Search all containers around the camp after
  > Can't carry everything? Leave and come back -- 7-day window open
  > Use Wagon overflow: dump heavy items, return for second haul


=======================================
 WHAT YOU'LL LOOT
=======================================

  Loot Type               | Notes
  ------------------------|----------------------------------------------
  Common Blueprint Tubes  | Main goal -- weapon and armor blueprints
  Uncommon Blueprint Tubes| Better variant -- weapon and armor blueprints
  Bronze / Iron Ingots    | Free crafting materials
  T2-T3 Tools/Weapons     | Break down at ranch or sell at Silverton
  Healing items           | Ointments, bandages -- restock your supply
  Silver Coins            | Random drop from bandits and chests
  Bandit's Stash Map      | Rare drop from boss -- reveals hidden hideout


=======================================
 TOP MISTAKES TO AVOID
=======================================

  Mistake                        | Consequence                 | Fix
  -------------------------------|-----------------------------|--------------------------
  Attack boss with bandits alive | Entire camp aggros at once  | Clear all grunts first
  Stand still during dynamite    | Massive damage, armor breaks| Keep moving constantly
  Go in below 70% durability     | Weapon breaks mid-fight     | Repair bench before going
  Full inventory going in        | Can't pick up loot          | Leave 15-20 slots empty
  Use ointments on regular mobs  | None left for boss = death  | Food only on grunts
  Ignore Anger Meter before trip | Ranch raided while away     | Check meter, stop at 75%
  Leave without second haul      | Missed free resources       | Use Wagon, come back


=======================================
 CAMP RESET SCHEDULE
=======================================

  > Camp resets EXACTLY 7 days after your first attack
  > You can re-enter multiple times within those 7 days
  > Use Day 1 for main clear + boss kill
  > Use Day 2-3 for leftover loot hauls (if inventory was full)
  > Day 7: reset -- bandits respawn, chests refresh -- repeat

  RECOMMENDED WEEKLY RHYTHM:
    Day 1 -- Full clear run (boss + all loot)
    Day 2 -- Second loot haul if needed (Wagon run)
    Day 7 -- Reset day: plan next clear for this day going forward


=======================================
 TL;DR -- STONE OUTPOST IN 4 STEPS
=======================================

  1. PREP    > Full T2 gear repaired + ointments + food + empty inventory
  2. CLEAR   > Pull bandits one by one from range, kill all before boss
  3. BOSS    > Hit-and-run, dodge dynamite, spike damage at <50% HP
  4. LOOT    > Boss chest first, then camp containers, Wagon for overflow
