T2 FIREARMS MASS PRODUCTION — CHEAT SHEET (Level 45)
=====================================================

Zone: Oak Forest (T2-3). At Lv.45 you have full access to Copper Mine, Tin Mine,
and Oak Forest — the exact resources needed for T2 bronze firearms.


=====================================================
 PRE-FLIGHT CHECKLIST
=====================================================

  Requirement                    | How to Check
  -------------------------------|----------------------------------------------
  Gun Workbench Lv.2             | Tap bench > Upgrade > T2 recipes unlocked
  Foundry Lv.2                   | Required for Bronze Ingots — upgrade FIRST
  Pepperbox Blueprint            | Gun Workbench menu > Pistols section
  Bronze Weapon Parts blueprint  | Default unlock at Gun Workbench Lv.2
  Bronze Plate blueprint         | Foundry menu — unlocked at Foundry Lv.2
  Wagon ready + 3-4 pickaxes     | For bulk ore hauls
  Storage chests cleared         | Free up space before each run

  WARNING: Floor tiles under Gun Workbench AND Foundry must be upgraded
  to matching tier or the Upgrade button stays greyed out.


=====================================================
 FULL MATERIAL CHAIN (Pepperbox — Best T2 Gun at Lv.45)
=====================================================

  Pepperbox (T2 Pistol)
  ├── 2x Bronze Weapon Parts  --+
  ├── 1x Bronze Plate          | all made from Bronze Ingots
  └── 1x Bronze Rivet          --+

  1x Bronze Ingot (Foundry Lv.2):
      = 4x Copper Ore
      + 4x Tin Ore
      + 2x Brown Coal

  Raw Ore Needed Per Gun:

  Material      | Per 1 Gun | Per 10 Guns | Farm Location
  --------------|-----------|-------------|-------------------------
  Copper Ore    | ~16-20    | ~180        | Copper Mine (T1 zone)
  Tin Ore       | ~16-20    | ~180        | Tin Mine (T2 zone)
  Brown Coal    | ~8-10     | ~90         | Any mine (spawns alongside ore)

  TIP: Stockpile ALL raw ore first, then smelt in one overnight batch.
  Queuing in bulk is faster than crafting 1-2 guns per day.


=====================================================
 FARMING ROUTES (Energy-Optimized)
=====================================================

  Route A — Pure Ore Run (Fastest bronze stockpile)
  ---------------------------------------------------
  Ranch
    > Copper Mine  (fill inventory: Copper Ore + Brown Coal)
    > Tin Mine     (fill remaining space: Tin Ore)
    > Ranch
    > Dump all into Foundry queue > sleep > smelt overnight

  Route B — Stacked XP Run (Farm + Progress)
  -------------------------------------------
  Ranch
    > Oak Forest   (bandits for XP + quests + Oak timber)
    > Tin Mine     (grab Tin on return trip)
    > Silverton    (trade surplus furs/hides for Silver Coins)
    > Ranch

  Route C — Trade Shortcut (Low-energy or busy day)
  --------------------------------------------------
  Silverton > Metals Merchant: 5x Copper > 1x Bronze Ingot equivalent
            > Native Tribe:    Wolf Teeth / Bear Claws > Tin Ore
  Ranch     > smelt whatever you traded for > craft guns
             (skip mining entirely on busy days)

  KEY TIP: Inside mines, always use pickaxes you FIND on the ground first.
  Saves your crafted pickaxes' durability for later.


=====================================================
 DAILY PRODUCTION LOOP
=====================================================

  -- LOGIN --------------------------------------------------------
  1. Start Foundry: queue ALL Copper + Tin + Coal > Bronze Ingots

  -- WHILE FOUNDRY RUNS -------------------------------------------
  2. Use full energy on 1 ore run (Route A preferred)
  3. Return > smelt new ore > add to queue

  -- BEFORE SLEEP -------------------------------------------------
  4. Craft: Bronze Weapon Parts, Bronze Plates, Bronze Rivets
  5. Queue Gun Workbench with as many Pepperboxes as mats allow
  6. Log out > everything crafts passively overnight

  -- NEXT LOGIN ---------------------------------------------------
  7. Collect finished guns > store or sell > repeat from step 1

  Overnight Queue Priority Order:
    1st > Foundry        (slowest, queue longest batches)
    2nd > Components     (Weapon Parts, Plates, Rivets)
    3rd > Gun Workbench  (fastest, queue last before sleep)


=====================================================
 BLUEPRINT SOURCES & RARITY SYSTEM
=====================================================

  IMPORTANT: All T2 gun rarities use the SAME crafting materials
  (Bronze Ingots + components). Rarity is in the BLUEPRINT only —
  better blueprint = higher damage, durability, and bonus stats.

  ── RARITY GRADES & WHERE TO FIND THEM ────────────────────────────

  Grade      | Tube Type        | Where to Get the Tube
  -----------|------------------|----------------------------------------------
  Common     | Common Tube      | Gun Workbench Lv.2 default / Ranch Guide
  Uncommon   | Uncommon Tube    | Gustav (Silverton) — trade Common > Uncommon
             |                  | Oak Forest chest loot / bandit drops
  Rare       | Rare Tube        | Bandit Camps (Oak Forest) — clear camps fully
             |                  | Smuggler Adam McCoy (Silverton) — daily stock
             |                  | Bounty hunt completions / specific side quests
  Epic       | Industrial Tube  | Smuggler Adam McCoy (primary source)
             |                  | Build Dovecote at ranch to unlock McCoy access
             |                  | Season Pass milestones (late tiers)
             |                  | Alliance Merchant's Shop (if built)

  ── SPECIFIC T2 FIREARM BLUEPRINT SOURCES ──────────────────────────

  Weapon                | Rarity   | Best Source
  ----------------------|----------|------------------------------------------
  Pepperbox (Pistol)    | Common   | Gun Workbench Lv.2 default
  Pepperbox             | Uncommon | Gustav: Common > Uncommon tube trade
  Pepperbox             | Rare     | Bandit Camp loot (Oak Forest)
  Pepperbox             | Epic     | Adam McCoy / Season Pass late milestone
  Bronze Rifle          | Common   | Frontier Tube (Oak Forest chests)
  Bronze Rifle          | Rare+    | Adam McCoy / Bandit Camp Rare Tubes
  Bronze Shotgun        | Common   | Adam McCoy daily stock (check rotation)
  Bronze Shotgun        | Rare+    | Adam McCoy (Rare Tube rotation)
  Bronze Spear          | Common   | Frontier Tube (Oak Forest / Ranch Guide)

  ── DROP LOCATIONS — DETAILED ──────────────────────────────────────

  Location               | What It Drops          | How Often
  -----------------------|------------------------|------------------------
  Oak Forest Bandit Camps| Common + Uncommon Tubes| Every clear (respawns)
  Oak Forest Chests      | Frontier Tubes (random)| Random loot table
  Silverton (Gustav)     | Upgrades Common > Unc. | Daily reset — 1 trade
  Adam McCoy (Silverton) | Rare + Industrial Tubes| Daily rotation — check
  Ranch Guide objectives | Various Tubes          | Complete specific tasks
  Shootout Spots (event) | Rare Tube possible     | Timed event — map icon
  Bounty Hunt rewards    | Rare Tube              | After bounty completion
  Season Pass milestones | Epic Tubes             | Free tier (late rewards)
  In-game Shop           | All tube types         | Free daily offer exists

  ── HOW TO CHECK A SPECIFIC GUN'S DROP SOURCE IN-GAME ──────────────

  1. Open Gun Workbench > find the gun you want
  2. If blueprint not learned > tap "Where to find?" button
  3. Game shows exact tube type needed + where to get it
  4. Use this for any weapon — always current and accurate

  ── STAT DIFFERENCES BY RARITY (T2 approximate) ────────────────────

  Rarity    | Damage   | Durability | Bonus stat
  ----------|----------|------------|------------------------------------------
  Common    | Base     | Base       | None
  Uncommon  | +10-15%  | +10%       | None or minor
  Rare      | +20-30%  | +20%       | 1 active bonus (crit, enemy type damage)
  Epic      | +40-50%  | +30%       | 1-2 strong bonuses + best-in-tier stats

  ── BONUS STAT TYPES ON RARE/EPIC T2 GUNS ──────────────────────────

  Bonus Type              | Effect                        | Best For
  ------------------------|-------------------------------|-------------------
  +Crit Chance            | % chance to deal double hit   | Bandit farming XP
  +Damage vs. Bandits     | More damage to humanoid enemies| Oak Forest runs
  +Damage vs. Animals     | More damage to wildlife        | Hide farming
  +Attack Speed           | Faster fire rate               | All combat
  +Headshot Bonus         | Extra damage on headshots      | Pistols/Rifles

  ── BLUEPRINT LEVELING ──────────────────────────────────────────────

  - Find a DUPLICATE blueprint tube (same gun, same rarity)
  - Go to Gun Workbench > select gun > tap Upgrade Blueprint
  - Pay Silver cost > stats permanently improve on all future crafts
  - T2 blueprint upgrades cost SILVER ONLY (no rare mats needed)
  - Max blueprint level = maximum stats for that rarity grade

  ── RARITY PRIORITY UNLOCK ORDER ────────────────────────────────────

  1. Common Pepperbox (default) — craft immediately, use as base
  2. Uncommon Pepperbox via Gustav — daily trade, fast free upgrade
  3. Rare Pepperbox from Bandit Camps — farm Oak Forest camps daily
  4. Rare Bronze Rifle from Adam McCoy — check his daily rotation
  5. Epic variants — only via Season Pass or McCoy; don't grind for these

  NOTE: Never skip Bandit Camp clears in Oak Forest — they are the
  single best free source of Rare Tube drops at this level range.

 SKILL TREE — Craftsman Nodes to Unlock at Lv.45

  -----------------|---------------------------------------------------

=====================================================
 SELL vs. KEEP DECISIONS
=====================================================

  Situation                    | Action
  -----------------------------|----------------------------------------------
  More guns than you need      | Trade at Silverton > Silver Coins
  Guns at <30% durability      | Use Repair Bench — cheaper than re-crafting
  Season Pass active           | Check "craft X weapons" task > Golden Bullets
  In an Alliance               | Donate surplus > Alliance Points
  No blueprint yet             | Farm Oak Forest bandit camps — T2 tube drops


=====================================================
 TOP GOTCHAS
=====================================================

  Mistake                      | Consequence                  | Fix
  -----------------------------|------------------------------|-----------------------------
  Foundry below Lv.2           | Can't smelt Bronze — stuck   | Upgrade Foundry BEFORE ore
  Not enough Brown Coal        | Smelting stalls mid-batch    | Carry 2x more coal always
  Anger Meter maxes at mine    | Surprise raid when offline   | Stop at ~75% Anger, go home
  Inventory full on return     | Wasted trip, dropped mats    | Park Wagon near mine entry
  Wrong blueprint tube Gustav  | Wasted Uncommon tube         | Only give COMMON tubes
  Levelling too fast           | T3 enemies before T2 ready   | Only do farming-tied quests


=====================================================
 QUICK NUMBERS CARD
=====================================================

  1 Pepperbox      ~ 4-5 Bronze Ingots (all parts combined)
  10 Pepperboxes   ~ 180 Copper + 180 Tin + 90 Coal (raw)

=====================================================
 TL;DR — THE 3-STEP MASS PRODUCTION FORMULA
=====================================================

  1. FARM    > Route A daily: Copper Mine > Tin Mine > Ranch
  2. QUEUE   > Foundry first, Components second, Gun Workbench last (overnight)
  3. COLLECT > Grab finished guns, sell or store, repeat
