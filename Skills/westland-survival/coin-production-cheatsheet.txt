SILVER COIN MASS PRODUCTION — CHEAT SHEET (All Levels)
========================================================

Silver Coins are the PRIMARY in-game currency used for trading, energy
restores, crafting speed-ups, skill unlocks, and the Barber.
This guide covers every reliable method to earn and stack coins fast.


========================================================
 PRE-FLIGHT CHECKLIST
========================================================

  Requirement                  | Why It Matters
  -----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------
  Silverton access             | Main marketplace — resets every 24h, sell here
  Active Alliance membership   | Multiplies reward income; Canyon Trials access
  5 active Tracks (Skill Tree) | Each track generates Books + Silver rewards
  Daily quests visible         | Primary guaranteed coin source every session
  Craftsman nodes unlocked     | Trade Perk = better exchange rates at merchants
  Wagon available              | Bulk resource hauls for trade surplus production
  Furs, Hides, Fangs, Claws    | High-value trade items — hoard these, don't sell cheap

  TIP: Check daily quests BEFORE choosing your farming route.
  Overlapping objectives = double income from the same trip.


========================================================
 SILVER COIN INCOME SOURCES — RANKED BY RELIABILITY
========================================================

  Rank | Source                    | Coins/Day   | Notes
  -----|---------------------------|-------------|----------------------------------
  1    | Daily Quests              | High        | Complete ALL of them, every day
  2    | Daily Login Reward        | Moderate    | Compounds over streak — never miss
  3    | Silverton Merchant Trades | High        | Sell surplus resources to merchants
  4    | Canyon Trials             | High        | Alliance required; big payout
  5    | Watching Ads              | Low-Mod     | Free, stack daily to add up
  6    | VIP / Super VIP Bonus     | Moderate    | If subscribed, collect daily

  9    | Alliance Activities       | Variable    | Participate actively for rewards

  RULE: NEVER skip daily quests or daily login — these two alone
  can sustain solid coin income for F2P players indefinitely.


========================================================
 SILVERTON MERCHANT TRADE GUIDE (Resets Every 24h)
========================================================

  What to SELL for Silver:

  Merchant         | Sell These            | Notes
  -----------------|-----------------------|----------------------------------
  Fur Trader       | Furs, Hides           | Check rate daily — best passive sell
  Metals Merchant  | Surplus ore/ingots    | Use 5 Copper > 1 Bronze trick to upgrade
  Tanner           | Leather + backpacks   | If you have excess from farming
  Blacksmith       | Spare tools, ingots   | Sell extras you won't craft with
  Prospector       | Any resources         | Converts to Gold Nuggets (keep or trade)

  What to HOARD and NOT sell cheap:

  Item             | Why Keep
  -----------------|-----------------------------------------
  Fangs & Claws    | Trade at HIGH value at northern merchants
  Wolf Teeth       | Tin Ore exchange at Native Tribe
  Bear Claws       | Strong merchant value, don't dump early
  Buffalo Fish     | Used for gear repair — worth more than coins
  Blueprint Tubes  | Trade Common > Uncommon at Gustav (Silverton)

  TIP: Merchant stock resets at the same time every 24 hours.
  Check Silverton every single day — favorable rates appear randomly
  and can let you convert low-value mats into significant coin.


========================================================
 DAILY COIN FARMING ROUTE (Energy-Optimized)
========================================================

  Route A — Full Coin Day (Ranch-Based Player)
  ---------------------------------------------
  Ranch
    > Collect Field / Well / Chicken Coop production
    > Check daily quests — note ALL objectives
    > Oak Forest    (kill bandits: XP + loot + quest kills)
    > Tin Mine      (ore for crafting surplus to sell)
    > Silverton     (sell surplus furs/hides/leather/ores)
                    (trade with Gustav — Common > Uncommon tube)
                    (collect daily coin quests if in Silverton)
    > Ranch         (craft items needed for quests, repair gear)

  Route B — Speed Run (Low Energy Day)
  -------------------------------------
  Ranch
    > Silverton     (sell yesterday's stockpile, collect daily reset)
    > Watch all available ads (partial energy + bonus coins)
    > Ranch         (craft 1 quest item, submit quest for coins)

  Route C — Canyon Trials Day (Alliance Members Only)
  ----------------------------------------------------
  Ranch
    > Complete Canyon Trials             (high Silver payout)
    > Silverton merchant trades          (sell surplus)
    > Return Ranch > log quests done     (collect quest Silver)

  TIP: Always combine merchant trading + quest completion +
  ad watching in one Silverton trip. Never make separate visits.


========================================================
 DAILY MUST-DO COIN ACTIONS (In Order)
========================================================

  -- LOGIN -------------------------------------------------------
  1. Collect daily login reward (coins + resources)
  2. Watch ALL available ads immediately (refresh every few hours)
  3. Open daily quests — read ALL objectives before planning route

  -- DURING SESSION ----------------------------------------------
  4. Run farming route (Route A preferred)
  5. Sell surplus at Silverton merchants
  6. Visit Gustav — trade Common blueprint tube > Uncommon
  7. Check Canyon Trials if Alliance is active
  8. Submit completed daily quests for Silver

  -- BEFORE LOGOUT -----------------------------------------------
  9. Check 5 active Tracks are running (Book income = skill unlocks)
  10. Queue crafting for quest items needed tomorrow
  11. Monitor Anger Meter — don't leave full (raid risk)

  OVERNIGHT TIP: All passive ranch production (corn, water, eggs)
  accumulates while offline. Log in with a full stockpile to sell
  every morning = free coins no energy spent.


========================================================
 QUEST STACKING STRATEGY (Maximum Silver Per Hour)
========================================================

  The goal is to complete multiple quest objectives in a SINGLE trip.

  Step 1: Read all daily quests before leaving ranch
  Step 2: Group quests by zone:
          - "Kill X bandits"    > Oak Forest (highest bandit density)
          - "Collect X ore"     > Copper/Tin Mine (same route)
          - "Visit Silverton"   > Done on return leg automatically
          - "Craft X items"     > Do at ranch after farming leg
  Step 3: Plot one route that hits all zones
  Step 4: Submit all quests at once when back at ranch

  BONUS: Stack Track tasks that align with quests
         Example: Track says "craft 5 weapons" + Quest says "craft 3 weapons"
         = complete both from ONE crafting session
         = earn Books + Silver simultaneously

========================================================
 CANYON TRIALS — HIGHEST SINGLE SILVER SOURCE
========================================================

  Requirements:
  - Active Alliance membership
  - Appropriate gear for trial tier

  How to maximize:
  - Participate in ALL Canyon Trials your Alliance schedules
  - Don't skip even if gear isn't perfect — partial completions count
  - Use Canyon Trials rewards to fund Skill Tree unlocks (not energy)

  TIP: Canyon Trials Silver income alone can cover daily speed-ups,
  merchant purchases, and skill resets without touching farm loot.


========================================================
 SEASON PASS — PASSIVE COIN ENGINE
========================================================

  Currency: Golden Bullets (from daily + seasonal tasks)

  How it feeds Silver Coins:
  1. Complete ALL daily season tasks > earn Golden Bullets
  2. Progress Season Pass milestones > unlock Silver Coin rewards
  3. "Craft X weapons / Kill X bandits" tasks overlap with farm routes
     = earn Golden Bullets while doing your normal daily loop
  4. Season rewards at later milestones include large Silver drops

  TIP: Free tier Season Pass is enough to earn substantial Silver.
  You do NOT need Premium to profit from the coin rewards.


========================================================
 TOP COIN MISTAKES TO AVOID
========================================================

  Mistake                          | Consequence               | Fix
  ---------------------------------|---------------------------|---------------------------
  Selling Fangs & Claws too early  | Lost high-value trades    | Hoard until Silverton check
  Missing daily quest deadline     | Permanent coin loss       | Set a daily reminder/alarm
  Spending Silver on energy        | Drains reserves fast      | Use ads + food for energy
  Not running 5 Tracks             | Lost Book & Silver income | Fill all 5 Track slots
  Skipping Canyon Trials           | Missed biggest payout     | Coordinate with Alliance
  Buying from merchants (not sell) | Coins go out not in       | Sell surplus, don't buy
  Ignoring Anger Meter             | Raid steals your resources| Farm at <75% Anger only
  Not stacking quest objectives    | Wasted trips, less Silver | Read quests before routing

  CRITICAL: Never spend Silver Coins on energy restores unless it's
  an emergency during a timed event. Silver is too valuable a currency
  to burn on energy when ads and food exist as free alternatives.
